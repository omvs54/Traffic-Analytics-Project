Phase 1: Data Engineering

The foundation of the analysis involved cleaning raw data and designing a robust Star Schema for efficient querying.

Data Cleaning Pipeline (Python)

Handled missing values using median imputation and standardized timestamp formats.

# Python Snippet: Preprocessing & Enrichment
df['DateTime'] = pd.to_datetime(df['DateTime'], errors='coerce')
df['Vehicles'] = pd.to_numeric(df['Vehicles'], errors='coerce')

# Imputing missing values
df['Vehicles'].fillna(df['Vehicles'].median(), inplace=True)

# Feature Engineering for Drill-Downs

Phase 4: Strategic Solutions (Case Study)

Based on the data analysis, the following recommendations were proposed to the traffic management committee.

Problem: Evening Bottleneck at Junction 1

Junction 1 exceeds capacity by 15% between 17:00 and 19:00, causing ripple effects across the downtown grid.

Proposed Actions

Short-Term (Immediate)

Implement Dynamic Signal Timing. Extend green light duration for Northbound lanes by 20 seconds during the 17:00-19:00 window.

Medium-Term

Deploy traffic marshals during Rainy weather forecasts, as data shows a 10% drop in flow speed during precipitation.

Long-Term

Initiate infrastructure review to widen the turn lane at Junction 1, as volume growth is projected to continue.
