Exploratory Data Analysis: Traffic Patterns

Date: October 26, 2023

Objective: To identify congestion hotspots, peak traffic hours, and general traffic trends using historical sensor data.

1. Peak Traffic Hours

Key Insight: Traffic volume peaks significantly during evening rush hours (17:00 - 19:00), with a secondary, smaller peak in the morning (08:00).

3. Data Notes & Limitations
